#1 Why I Almost Didn’t Start This Blog (And Why I Did Anyway)

Hi everyone. Parinita here. My hands are literally shaking as I post this. I am a recent grad, and to be honest, adulting is hard. I’ve been applying for jobs, but I can’t help but miss being a kid.

I have been reading since as long as I can remember. It started with books from Disney princess stories to classics like Lord of the Rings. Never got into non-fiction though, they can be real snooze fests.

I have always been inspired by writers. I think it's completely amazing how they create a different world by their imagination– J.K. Rowling, Cassandra Clare. I think I can be a good writer. I have been applying for jobs as a technical content writer.

I wrote 2 books - or tried to - one was a murder mystery and another was an apocalyptic world with zombies.

Writing at times is easy to me, but the publishing part makes me nervous. Letting people read what you write is like handing them a piece of yourself — and people can be judgmental. They may not always like what you write, they may not agree with you. But that’s ok. It’s part of the process.

I like to think people have much better things to do than think about what I write. It gives me a sense of peace. It’s ok to not be perfect, all that matters is to start somewhere.

I think of myself as my own audience. If I like it, someone else out there probably will too. Maybe even more people.

I’m not sure which direction this blog will go. There are so many things I like, so many new things to learn. Hopefully I’ll get the hang of it and I’ll keep getting better. So stay tuned.

Thanks for reading.
